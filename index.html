IDX Listing Pages — Standard Architecture
Thousands of property detail pages (/property-search/detail/) are normal IDX behavior for a Sierra Interactive real estate site. These are dynamically generated, ephemeral pages that appear and disappear as listings come on and off market. They do not represent a content risk — they’re standard infrastructure.
Neighborhood Pages — Build Authority Hubs
The real optimization opportunity: ~500+ thin community template pages could be consolidated into 30–50 authoritative neighborhood hub pages with unique video tours, agent commentary, original photography, and hyper-local market data. Each hub becomes a ranking powerhouse instead of dozens of thin pages competing with each other.
Off-Topic Blog Content — Candidates for Pruning
Several blog posts attract traffic but not Denver buyer/seller leads: Miami neighborhoods (1,387 sessions), Sebring FL living (858 sessions), lighted mirror repairs (941 sessions), junk removal in Honolulu (3,512 GSC impressions). These pages dilute topical authority. Consider redirecting or consolidating into Denver-focused content that drives local leads.
